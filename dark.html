The Story

Love at First Swipe

It started the way so many modern love stories do — a swipe, a match, a spark. But what began on Tinder quickly leapt across borders and time zones, from quick messages to marathon Skype calls that stretched deep into the night. She was in Colombia, he was worlds away, but the distance only made the connection fiercer. There were no numbers between zero and ten — it was all or nothing from the start.

Where two paths became one
Crossing Mountains

When they finally met in person, the world clicked into place. Laura brought the warmth of Colombia — her family, her roots, a father figure named Dr. Anthony who shaped the woman Josh fell in love with. Josh brought the pull of adventure — the open trail, the next peak, the dog at his side who'd become their first shared love. Together, they climbed. Literally and in every other way.

Every summit, earned together
She never stopped chasing the horizon
Building a Life

They built a home between cultures, between languages, between the familiar and the unknown. A beach wedding sealed what they already knew. Adventures stacked up — new cities, new challenges, costumes at parties, holidays with the dogs, quiet mornings with the cat. Ten years of choosing each other, every single day.

The day the ocean held its breath
A Decade of Adventure

Now, ten years in, the view from the mountaintop is breathtaking. Not because the climbing stopped — but because they never stopped climbing together. This is their story. A story of swipes and Skype calls, of Colombia and new beginnings, of ropes and ridges, of a dog who was there for all of it. Ten years, drawn to life.
