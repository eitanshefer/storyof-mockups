What We Do

Stories Worth Telling

We create personalized animated films for life's most meaningful moments. From weddings and anniversaries to memorials and milestone birthdays, each film is a hand-crafted work of art.

Our team of animators, illustrators, and storytellers work closely with you to transform your memories, photographs, and family lore into a cinematic experience that will be treasured for generations.

About StoryOf

We bring personal journeys to life through cutting-edge animation techniques. Our team celebrates the magic in everyday experiences, revives moments lost to time, and strengthens the bonds that tie us all together.

With over 15 years of experience in the international creative industry, we've crafted award-winning animated stories that have captivated global audiences.

I founded StoryOf to bring people together through creativity. Our stories connect us to our roots and to one another, and make this world a bit brighter. When we're creating a film, we don't just save the memories: we're building bridges between hearts.
Daniella Koffler, Founder & CEO
