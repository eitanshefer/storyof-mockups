More Than a Film.

About Us

The Studio Behind
the Stories

StoryOf is a boutique animation studio founded on a simple belief: every person's story deserves to be told beautifully. We work with families around the world to capture their most meaningful moments and transform them into hand-illustrated animated films.

What sets us apart isn't just the art — it's the process. Daniella's gift for human connection means the interview itself becomes a transformative experience. Families rediscover stories they'd forgotten, hear perspectives they'd never known, and come together in ways they didn't expect.

DK
“I founded StoryOf because I believe the most important stories are the ones we tell each other — around the table, between generations, from the heart.”
Daniella Koffler, Founder & CEO
